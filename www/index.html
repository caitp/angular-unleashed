Performance

Bottlenecks:

  • Dirty-checking / Scope Events
  • DOM manipulation causing reflows
  • Initial compilation of markup

Scopes have been enhanced in a few ways

  • New ::bind-once expressions
  • Under the hood improvements to core algorithms
  • Shrink the data-set and spend less time churning when $digest is called.
